I'm Tessa Vujovic.
Product designer based in Cape Town and Europe.

After completing my studies in interior architecture, I began my career at an interior architecture and design firm, creating spaces for commercial wine farms, residential homes, and eco-friendly yachts. During COVID, I transitioned into digital product design, embracing remote work and joining the award-winning agency MakeReign .

Today, as the Lead Product Designer at KoboToolbox, I design intuitive digital tools that empower humanitarian organizations worldwide.

When I’m not traveling, you’ll probably find me in Kalk Bay, coffee and pastry in hand, pretending I’m training for a half marathon.
