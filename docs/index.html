Hello, I'm Tessa — a product designer originally from South Africa.

Today, as the Lead Product Designer at Kobo Toolbox, I design intuitive digital tools that empower humanitarian organizations worldwide.

When I'm not traveling, you'll find me sipping coffee at a cozy spot in Kalk Bay, watching the world pass by.
